What is a SIP?

A Systematic Investment Plan (SIP) is a disciplined way to invest a fixed amount in mutual funds every month. It helps you build wealth gradually while taking advantage of compounding over time.

How to Use This SIP Calculator

  1. Enter the monthly investment amount.
  2. Provide the expected annual return percentage.
  3. Specify the investment tenure in years.
  4. Click the Calculate button.
  5. View your SIP’s future value and total invested amount instantly.

Example Calculation

For instance, if you invest ₹5,000 per month at an expected annual return of 12% for 10 years, the calculator will show your total investment and expected maturity value at the end of the tenure.
