Age Calculation Explained: Handling Leap Years & Time Zones

Age isn't just subtracting years—it's precise! Our tool uses Gregorian calendar rules: February has 29 days in leap years (divisible by 4, except centuries not by 400). For time zones, we default to your browser's local time, but you can adjust the reference date. Example: Born in UTC but calculating in PST? Shift the ref date by hours.

Manual math tip: Years = ref year - birth year; adjust months/days if underflow (like your 30th birthday on Feb 28 non-leap year).
