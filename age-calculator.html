What is an Age Calculator?

An age calculator is a simple tool that lets you find your exact age based on your date of birth. It shows your age in years, months, and days within seconds. Whether you want to know your age for a form, event, or curiosity, this calculator makes it quick and effortless.

How to Use This Age Calculator

  1. Enter your date of birth in the first box.
  2. Optionally, choose a reference date to compare against.
  3. Click the Calculate Age button.
  4. See your age displayed instantly below the form.
