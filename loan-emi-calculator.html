What is an EMI?

An EMI (Equated Monthly Installment) is the fixed amount you pay every month to repay your loan over a specified period. It includes both principal and interest components. [1] [2]

EMI Formula

The EMI can be calculated using the formula:

EMI = P × r × (1 + r)^n / ((1 + r)^n – 1)

Where P = principal, r = monthly interest rate, n = total number of monthly payments. [3]
