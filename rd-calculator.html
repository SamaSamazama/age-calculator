What is a Recurring Deposit (RD)?

A Recurring Deposit (RD) is a simple savings tool where you deposit a fixed amount every month for a set tenure at a predetermined interest rate. It helps you build savings steadily while earning interest on your deposits.

How to Use This RD Calculator

  1. Enter your monthly deposit amount.
  2. Input the annual interest rate offered by your bank.
  3. Specify the tenure in years.
  4. Click the Calculate button.
  5. View your RD maturity amount and total deposits instantly.

Example Calculation

For example, if you deposit ₹5,000 every month at an annual interest rate of 6% for 5 years, the calculator will show your total maturity amount and total amount deposited.
