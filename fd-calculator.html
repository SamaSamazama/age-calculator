FD Calculator

What is a Fixed Deposit?

A Fixed Deposit (FD) is one of the safest investment options offered by banks and NBFCs. You deposit a lump sum for a specific tenure and earn a fixed interest rate. The longer the tenure, the higher your total return.

How to Use the FD Calculator

  1. Enter the amount you wish to invest (Principal).
  2. Input the annual interest rate offered by your bank.
  3. Enter the tenure in years.
  4. Select how often the interest is compounded (monthly, quarterly, yearly).
  5. Click Calculate to see your maturity amount and interest.

Example Calculation

If you invest ₹1,00,000 for 5 years at 7% annual interest compounded quarterly, the maturity amount will be approximately ₹1,41,478. The total interest earned will be ₹41,478.
